The hardworking and the lazy man

Chapter 1:The lazy man

Once upon a time there lived a lazy man who loved to sleep and hated work. He lived in a house he owned through his father's properties. He was always found dozing under trees, or eating in his neighbour's house. His favourite food is swallow and egusi soup. He is really known for his lies and dishonesty, he likes throwing fruits and bones into people's houses and lying. He is also known for his talented music which he sings for little children after they all have done their chores.He also likes collecting snacks from children and promising he will pay back.

Chapter 2:The hardworking man

There also lived a hardworking man who loved to work more than anything and only slept for 8 hours everyday. He works in his large farm that grows cassava,yam,carrots,lettuce,cabbage,rice,corn and maize. He works from 06:00am to 07:00pm. He is really known for his honesty,modesty and being helpful. He only eats two times a day, his favourite food is yam and fried egg. He also loves playing sport and he is really smart. He is also known for his great story-telling talents, he tells childrens stories everytime he comes backs from work, he is also a very good cook.

Chapter 3:The meeting

One day, while the hardworking man was coming home from work, he saw the lazy man singing to some little children. The hardworking man sat among the little crowd and listened to the song. After some minutes,when the lazy man was done, after everyone was gone the hardworking man walked up to the lazy man and they started talking. A few minutes later, they became friends. The hardworking man told the lazy man where he lived and told him to visit someday. The lazy man was happy and said he will visit the next evening. The next evening, the lazy man came to the hardworking man's house. The hardworking man welcomed him with open arms, he gave the lazy man some meat and tea. The lazy man enjoyed it and soon the both of them fell asleep.

Chapter 4:The little problem

One day, the lazy man came to visit the hardworking man. He told him that his wicked uncles came to collect the house from him and all the money his father left for him and that he had no-where to stay. He also asked if he could stay with him for a while till he can find a place to stay. The hardworking man said yes and he should move in now.When the lazy man moved in,he began to eat and sleep a lot and he was really messy. He could finish drinking tea and just throw the cup on the floor or he can just wake up and not arrange his bed but the hardworking man was always there to clean it up. One day after eating a whole pot of porridge, he just belched out loud and put the pot on the floor and went to sleep.The next day, the hardworking man went to the farm and asked some of the people working for him what he should do. Most of them said he should kick him out or he should disgrace him in front of the whole village but the hardworking man said he could tolerate him more longer. He went home feeling very tired. He told the lazy man that he could sing to earn money, the lazy man agreed but he only did it a few times and would just sneak away when the hardworking man was hard at work.

Chapter 5:Seperate ways

One day, the hardworking man caught the lazy man sneaking away and followed him. He saw him pluck an apple from a tree and eat it under a shady tree. The hardworking man kept calm and if he continues doing this he knew what he should do.Two days later, the hardworking man caught the lazy man collect some snacks from a 7 year old child and told him that if he told anyone, he will beat him up so badly he will enter a coma. After the child left,the hardworking man came out of his hiding spot and told him that he could not tolerate him more longer and he should pack out of his house.The lazy man packed out of his house in tears hoping he would cope.He lived under a old tree that only had 1 fruit.The lazy man suffered because he could not find food to eat and the neighbours he normally eats with wouldn't let him eat because they heard of his laziness. He couldn't earn money from singing because parents didn't want their children to stay around him because they didn't want them to be like him.Some days later, the lazy man went back to beg the hardworking man. And the hardworking man forgived him only if he promised to be hardworking.

Chapter 6:Friends again

After the lazy man returned to the hardworking man's house, he began to cry uncontrolably and begged for forgiveness. The hardworking man forgived the lazy man and they became friends once again.The next day, the lazy man went to apologise to all the childrens he had once collected from before. Everyone forgived him and he became hardworking and no one ever called him the lazy man he was now called the hardworking man 2.

Moral Lesson

1.Do not lie. 2.Do not be a lazy person.3.Always listen to hardworking people.4.Never collect children snacks.Note:No food for a lazy man
